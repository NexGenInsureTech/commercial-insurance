Strategic Imperative: Focus on Engineering

The path to profitable growth lies in Engineering Insurance, benefiting from a +21.2% growth surge, national infra push, and better pricing.

1. Align with National Priorities

Partner with 'Make in India' & infra agenda. Develop specialized products for national projects in transport, energy, and manufacturing.

2. Capitalize on Profitable Growth

Leverage the hardening market for underwriting discipline. Invest in technical expertise for superior risk assessment and value-based pricing.

3. Penetrate via Specialization

Build specialized B2B channels. Forge partnerships with EPC contractors, project finance lenders, and industrial bodies.

The 80/20 Blueprint: Winning the High-Value SME Market

Apply the Pareto Principle: focus on the vital 20% of SMEs that drive 80% of profitable growth. This interactive guide details the strategy.

1

Identify: Find the "Vital Few"

▸

Rigorously segment the market to find high-potential SMEs in sectors like Manufacturing, Exports, Tech, and specialized Services. Analyze your own portfolio to find existing profitable clusters and create a data-backed "Ideal SME Customer Profile".

Example: Ideal SME Profile

Company: "Precision Auto Components Pvt. Ltd."
Location: Pune/Chakan industrial belt.
Profile: A tier-2 supplier to major automotive OEMs, specializing in high-precision machined parts. Employs 75 people, with advanced CNC machinery. Exports 30% of its output to Europe. Faces risks related to machinery breakdown, supply chain disruption, and product quality liability.

2

Innovate: Create Superior Value

▸

Develop a value proposition beyond standard policies. Create bundled "Business Protection Suites", offer "Risk Management as a Service" (RaaS) with checklists and webinars, and simplify the user experience with digital journeys and dedicated claims support.

Example: Product Innovation

Product: "Manufacturing Shield+" Bundle
Core Covers: Fire & Special Perils, Machinery Breakdown, Burglary.
Value-Add Covers: Product Liability (basic limit), Marine Transit (for exports), Business Interruption (following machinery breakdown).
Service: Complimentary annual thermographic audit of electrical panels to prevent fire risk.

3

Distribute: Engineer an Efficient Model

▸

Engineer a high-efficiency distribution model. Empower specialist commercial brokers, forge B2B alliances with SME-focused lenders and industry associations, and de-emphasize mass aggregators that drive competition solely on price.

Example: Distribution Channel

Partnership: Alliance with a mid-sized bank specializing in SME business loans.
Model: When the bank approves a machinery loan for an SME like "Precision Auto Components," our "Manufacturing Shield+" policy is offered as a pre-underwritten, integrated part of the loan package. The bank's relationship manager is trained to explain the benefits, and the process is digitized for instant policy issuance.

4

Measure: Track What Matters

▸

Implement a data-driven performance loop. Move beyond GWP to focus on KPIs like Combined Ratio per SME cluster, Customer Lifetime Value (CLV), cross-sell ratios, and retention rates. Establish formal feedback loops for continuous improvement.

Example: Performance KPI Dashboard

Segment: Auto Component SMEs - Pune Cluster
KPIs to Track:
- Combined Ratio: Target < 95%
- Avg. Policies per Client: Target > 2.5
- 1-Year Retention Rate: Target > 90%
- % of Clients using RaaS: Target > 30%
