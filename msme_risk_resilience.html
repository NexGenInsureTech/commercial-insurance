The MSME Insurance Imperative

From Reactive Safeguard to Proactive Growth Engine

MSMEs are the backbone of the Indian economy, contributing 30% to GDP and employing over 110 million people. Yet, a staggering protection gap leaves them vulnerable. This interactive report explores how to transform insurance from a perceived cost into a strategic asset for resilience and growth.

The Glaring Protection Gap

Despite their critical economic role, the vast majority of MSMEs operate without a financial safety net, making them acutely vulnerable to shocks. This is not just an Indian issue, but a global challenge in developing economies.

India's Underinsurance Crisis

Only a tiny fraction of India's 63 million MSMEs have adequate insurance, exposing the economy to systemic risk.

Barriers to Adoption: India vs. Global Peers

MSMEs in India and other developing nations face similar hurdles, with cost perception and complexity being primary deterrents.

The Growth Enabler: Insurance Beyond Protection

Unlocking Credit: The Virtuous Cycle

Lenders view well-insured businesses as less risky. Keyman insurance, in particular, directly mitigates 'key person risk,' often a requirement for business loans. This creates a powerful cycle where insurance unlocks capital for growth.
